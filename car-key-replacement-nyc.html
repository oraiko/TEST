What to have ready

Speed up your replacement

Having a few details on hand means a faster quote and a shorter visit:

  • Proof of ownership — registration or title, plus a photo ID. We match the key to a vehicle you own.
  • Year, make & model — and the trim if you know it; it affects the key type and programming.
  • The VIN — found on the dashboard by the windshield or the driver's door jamb. It helps us prep the correct key.
  • Any existing key — even a broken one speeds cutting and can lower the cost.

Pro tip: if you still have one working key, ask for a spare now. Cutting a duplicate while a good key exists is quicker and cheaper than an all-key-lost job at 11 p.m. on the FDR.
